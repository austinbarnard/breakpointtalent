About BreakPoint Talent

The Personal Touch, Without the Wait

BreakPoint Talent is an Ohio-based boutique recruiting firm built around one simple idea: people matter most. We care deeply about the companies we partner with and the candidates we represent. Behind every open role is a team trying to grow, and behind every candidate is a person looking to build something better for themselves.

Every search we run is handled with full attention and personal care. We speak with every candidate ourselves. We craft every submittal individually. There are no handoffs, no automated outreach on behalf of our clients, no shortcuts that compromise quality. What modern recruiting technology gives us is time, and we put that time back into the relationships and conversations that actually make a great hire happen.

With nearly 10 years of full-desk recruiting experience across Accounting & Finance, Legal, Technology, Manufacturing, and more, we bring the market knowledge to move with speed and the judgment to know when something is truly the right fit and not just close enough. Our clients feel like we're part of their team. Our candidates feel like they have a real advocate.

We offer contingent and executive search solutions tailored to where you are and what you need.
