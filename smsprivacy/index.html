02

Purpose of SMS Notifications

BreakPoint Talent uses SMS notifications to communicate with clients and candidates in connection with our recruiting and staffing services. This includes:

  • Updates on active job searches and candidate submissions
  • Interview scheduling and coordination
  • Offer and placement notifications
  • Onboarding and billing communications for client companies
  • Important service updates and announcements

03

Opt-In and Consent

By providing your phone number and opting into our SMS notifications, you confirm that you are the owner or authorized user of the phone number provided. You consent to receive recurring automated text messages (SMS, MMS) from BreakPoint Talent regarding your active searches, service updates, offers, and other important notifications.

Consent is not required to make any purchase or engage our services. Message frequency may vary, but you can generally expect to receive between 1–5 messages per week.

04

Opt-Out Instructions

Your consent to receive automated texts is entirely voluntary. You may opt out at any time:

  • Reply STOP, END, CANCEL, UNSUBSCRIBE, or QUIT to any SMS message from us.
  • Email us at hello@breakpointtalent.com with your opt-out request.

After you opt out, you will receive a final confirmation message and no further messages will be sent to your number unless you re-enroll.
