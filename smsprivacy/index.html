BreakPoint Talent Online Information Practices

To better protect your privacy, we provide this notice explaining BreakPoint Talent's online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. This notice is easy to find — it is available on our homepage and whenever personally identifiable information may be requested.

Our Commitment to Privacy

BreakPoint Talent, operated by Kraig Talent LLC, is committed to protecting your personal information. Your data will be used solely for the purposes described in this policy — including managing recruiting engagements, processing payments, and ensuring proper communication regarding our services.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Mobile opt-in data and SMS consent will not be shared with any third parties.

The Information We Collect

When you browse our website or submit a form, we may collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, company, and the nature of your inquiry. This information is used to respond to your requests and improve our services.

We may also collect non-personally identifiable information such as browser type and pages visited, which helps us understand how our site is being used.

How We Use Your Information

BreakPoint Talent uses the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries from clients and candidates
  • Manage active recruiting searches and candidate submissions
  • Schedule interviews and coordinate placements
  • Process invoices and billing for client companies
  • Send service updates and important notifications

We will always give you the opportunity to opt out of direct marketing or market research communications.

Text Messaging (SMS/MMS)

If you opt in to receive text messages from BreakPoint Talent, we may collect and use your mobile phone number and messaging information (such as delivery confirmations and message content) to send you requested updates and to provide support. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

We do not sell or share your mobile number or SMS opt-in information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Opt-Out: You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message from us. You will receive a final confirmation and no further messages will be sent unless you re-enroll.

Help: Reply HELP to any message or email us at hello@breakpointtalent.com.

Consent: Consent to receive SMS is not required to make any purchase or engage our services. Message frequency may vary between 1–5 messages per week.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

BreakPoint Talent may share your personal data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third parties that help us deliver our services (platform providers, phone companies, messaging vendors). These providers may access personal information only to perform services for us and are not permitted to use it for their own marketing.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to confidentiality obligations.

All of the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent — this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. After this period, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

Compliance with Regulations

We comply with all relevant laws and regulations, including the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) and CTIA guidelines. Our practices ensure that you can easily manage your communication preferences at any time.

Your Acceptance of These Terms

By using BreakPoint Talent's website or services, you signify your agreement to this Privacy Policy. We reserve the right to change this policy at any time. Any revisions will be posted on this page. We encourage you to check this page periodically for updates.
